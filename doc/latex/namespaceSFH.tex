Determines an appropriate value for the star formation rate at a given age. 

The function looks for a representative value of the star formation rate given the age of the system, and takes into account an optional additional time delay. 
\begin{DoxyParams}{Parameters}
{\em age} & age of the system in Myr. \\
\hline
{\em redshift\+\_\+interpolator} & \doxylink{namespaceRedshiftInterpolator}{Redshift\+Interpolator} object that interpolates the redshift at a given age. \\
\hline
{\em Delta\+\_\+t} & time delay due to formation of binary or time required to reach the correct frequency bin, in Myr. \\
\hline
{\em SFH\+\_\+num} & which star formation history to select. 1\+: Madau \& Dickinson 2014, 2-\/4\+: made up, 5\+: constant 0.\+01. \\
\hline
{\em max\+\_\+z} & maximum redshift. \\
